Just checked <a href="http://www.orkut.co.in/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.orkut.co.in/', 'Indian version of Orkut']);" >Indian version of Orkut</a>. Its URL is <a href="http://www.orkut.co.in/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.orkut.co.in/', 'http://www.orkut.co.in/']);" title="http://www.orkut.co.in/">http://www.orkut.co.in/</a> as expected.

At first look I didn&#8217;t notice anything special. Maybe some features have been added internally.

Few things one can expect are&#8230;

  * Searches from <a href="http://www.orkut.co.in/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.orkut.co.in/', 'http://www.orkut.co.in/']);" title="http://www.orkut.co.in/">http://www.orkut.co.in/</a> will give priority to Indian users/communities.
  * Geotargeting of Orkut Apps. [Orkut Apps are officially available for][2] [India][3] & [Estonia][2] but can be used any orkut user by changing their country to India or Estonia. ([More details&#8230;][2])

There may be more to this but as I am [away for a week][4] can&#8217;t explore much. If you find anything great in Indian version, feel free to comment.
