Here is a list of video, audio and image formats supported by Format Factory:

**Video Formats:**

  * 3GP (.3gp)
  * MPEG-1 (.mpg)
  * Matroska (.mkv)
  * Flash Video (.flv)
  * MPEG-4 Part 14 (.mp4)
  * Audio Video Interleave (.avi)
  * Windows Media Video (.wmv)

**Audio Formats:**

  * MP3
  * OGG
  * Wave Sound (.wav)
  * Adaptive Multi-Rate (.amr)
  * Windows Media Audio (.wma)
  * Advanced Audio Coding (.aac)

**Image Formats:**

  * .JPG
  * .PNG
  * .ICO
  * .BMP
  * .GIF
  * .TIF
  * .TGA
