## Cheat Sheet for Google Translate to become into a Beatbox!

If you want to customize the beat, here is the key:

  * zk = suspended cymbal
  * bschk = snare
  * pv = brush
  * bk = bass
  * tk = flam1
  * vk = roll tap
  * kt = flam2
  * kttp = flam tap
  * krp = hi hat tap
  * pv = short roll
  * th = better hi hat
  * thp, ds = instant rimshot.
