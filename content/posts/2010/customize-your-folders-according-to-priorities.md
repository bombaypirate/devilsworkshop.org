Every day we create many folders on our computers, sometimes we get confused on looking the folders because all of them are alike with yellow color. So it is very easy to get confused with such a routine, and also we may assign priorities to some folders, then how to distinguish it from other folders ?

Do you fear that someone might unintentionally delete information necessary to you? Mark a folder, and other users will notice at once that the folder contains important information. For all this, FolderMarker is the Solution!

<a rel="attachment wp-att-21484" href="http://devilsworkshop.org/customize-your-folders-according-to-priorities/folder-marker-icon/"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-21484" title="folder marker icon" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/03/folder-marker-icon.png" alt="" width="360" height="115" /></a>

Foldermarker is a simple tool for Windows, which allows you to change the color and appearance of your folders just by one mouse click. It is available on right click context menu, just right click the folder and assign a color, you can also use your own custom folder icons.

[<img class="size-medium wp-image-21483 alignnone" style="border: 1px solid teal" title="folder marker" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/03/folder-marker-600x569.png" alt="" width="480" height="455" />][1]

**Features of FolderMarker** &#8211;

  * You can mark folders by priority (high, normal, low), by work status (done, half-done, planned) and information contained in the folder (work files, important files, temp files, private files).
  * You can customize the folders by context right click menu

[<img class="size-full wp-image-21481 alignnone" style="border: 1px solid teal" title="context menu" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/03/context-menu.png" alt="" width="474" height="459" />][2]

  * Not only colors, you can also assign letters, custom icons to the folders.

[<img class="size-full wp-image-21480 alignnone" style="border: 1px solid teal" title="alphabets" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/03/alphabets.png" alt="" width="267" height="227" />][3]

  * It can work with multiple folders at a time.
  * With one click you can apply colors or icons to all of its sub-folders.

[<img class="size-full wp-image-21479 alignnone" style="border: 1px solid teal" title="restore" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/03/restore.png" alt="" width="273" height="194" />][4]

  * You can also restore back to old color.
