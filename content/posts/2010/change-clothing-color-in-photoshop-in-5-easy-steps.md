In this tutorial we are going to learn how to change clothing color in Photoshop. Its easy and fun <img src="http://devilsworkshop.org/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;" />

<img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-32828" title="12" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/11/121-600x406.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="406" />

You can see the result in the image above.  
**Step** **1 **: Create a duplicate layer of the image (Press shortcut key** Ctrl+J**)**.**

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-32839" title="step-1" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/11/step-1.jpg" alt="" width="439" height="215" />

**Step 2 **: Select the &#8216;top&#8217; by lasso tool or pen tool which ever you prefer, here I have used lasso tool.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-32842" title="step-2" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/11/step-21.jpg" alt="" width="292" height="115" />

Make sure your selection is proper as shown in the below image.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-32843" title="step-3" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/11/step-3.jpg" alt="" width="291" height="399" />

**Step 3** : Now **create new ****fill** **or adjustment layer** option from layer control panel.  
(Refer image below) and select **Hue/saturation** option.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-32844" title="step-4" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/11/step-4.jpg" alt="" width="292" height="400" />

**Step 4 **: Hue/saturation window will appear. Set the Hue at (-113), Saturation at Â (+7), Lightness atÂ (-34) or you can play with the settings till you get your desired color.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-32852" title="step-5" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/11/step-5.jpg" alt="" width="290" height="300" />

**Step 5 **: Look for any holes or missing pieces. Hit **Ctrl+click** in the layer box.  
Fill in as many holes as you can with the **Brush Tool**.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-32853" title="step-6" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/11/step-6.jpg" alt="" width="302" height="277" /><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-32854" title="step-7" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/11/step-7.jpg" alt="" width="403" height="270" />

Make sure that the foreground color is white and background color is black.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-32855" title="step-8" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/11/step-8.jpg" alt="" width="272" height="331" />

Your Final image is now ready.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-32870" title="finalimage" src="http://cdn.devilsworkshop.org/files/2010/11/finalimage.jpg" alt="" width="291" height="400" />
